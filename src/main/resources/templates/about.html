About 3D Printer Shop inc.

Our Story

Our founder, Bob Mcgee has a passion for plastic. In his words "the world runs on plastic." In 2020,
he founded 3D Printer Shop inc. to cater to a new small-scale manufacturing industry: 3D print farms.
Due to breakdowns in the global supply chain, commercial purchasers were looking for suppliers who could
dynamically scale and adjust to make unique parts in specific sizes and quantities. While this new industry
grows and evolves, 3D Print Shop will always be standing by to offer quality supplies at stable prices.

Our Mission

3D Print Shop doesn't just supply large scale 3D print farms. Hobbyists and DIY types will find our prices
and quality beat everyone on the block. That's why we make our filaments in-house right here in Richmond, Virginia!
Our vertically integrated supply chain ensures that our prices and quality are stable and affordable - like plastic!
